Why we us?
Because we have made them dependent on us. Domesticated animals are animals that we took out of the wild to train them to do something to benefit us. When we did that, we took their ability to care for themselves away. They MUST be cared for. If they aren’t getting food, water, shelter, and proper medical care, then they die. From the viewpoint of people who have animals to help farm, or for transportation, a person’s success and ability to get work done is only as good as their animals are healthy.
